About Icon Computer Institute.

Icon Computer Training centre was born in a City named Harda. It was the 1st day of New Year 1415(It is known as ‘Poila Boishakh’). It took a membership of the world’s computer family. Icon started walking on the path of teaching with only two students. By degrees Icon started to become aged with various modern kinds of elements and day by day Icon started growing and introduces the new elements of computing to the students. In this way it also becomes to be renowned among the country and gain respective stage.
